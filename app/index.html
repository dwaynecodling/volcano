  • Legal & General

    The 'S(h)ift Happens' project was an Internal Communication Presentation produced for Legal & General for part of their 2014 Expo on Digital Strategy. Our brief was to design and create a creative response to a scripted piece of audio, the content of which involved raising L&G employee awareness of how changes in smart technology has impacted the way businesses communicate both internally and to customers.

    Final L&G Animation with Audio Boost (26 06 14).mov from Volcano Volcano on Vimeo.

    The team behind the project liked the idea of using animated-type to communicate mass information but were conscious, based on examples they had seen used in the past, that this could easily be over the top and distracting for their intended audience. For this reason, the strategy we adopted replied on the audio to tell the intended story with only key graphics and type used to help amplify and compliment the key values of an already strong script.

  • Amber Valley Flower & Garden Show

    The Amber Valley project was a hugely enjoyable project for us to work on. We are all massive fans of area and so were over the moon when Derbyshire in Bloom approached us to produce promotional material for their first annual flower & garden show. Due to target demographic and the fact it was a springtime event, we felt it was important to convey a vintage look and feel that adhered to the events more mature in years whilst catching the eye of a potential younger clientele. This gave us a window of opportunity to stray away from the typical photography lead promotions and showcase more our illustrative skillset by producing a number of digitally painted components which could be utilised on a wide range of material from posters to social media feeds.
